MAKEUP BROWSER

About:

MakeUp Browser is an ongonig passion project of mine that stemmed for my love of passionate online communities and makeup. Currently, there exists a strong makeup community distributed online, but there isn't a common meeting group specifically devoted to them and their work. I want to create a community with makeup ethusiasts can share images of new looks as well as information.

I'm also inpsired by Instagram's seamless user experience of uploading images and looking through tons of content. I think one of the challenges of creating online communities is figuring how to drive users back to your website everyday. One of my produest features was adding the ability to preview images after uploading an image.
