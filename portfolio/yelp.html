YELP

About:

I love food. More often than not, my friends and I would make plans for dinner, but we'll procrastinate on deciding where to eat. I created a web app that takes away the responsiblity of decision making by generating a highly-rated restaurant choice (4 stars or above) for indecisive people.

This app was my first attempt at using popular APIs. I choose Yelp because it seemed to have the most documentation. I also wrote about my experience in my blog.

After completing this app, I shared it with my friends and family. While my friends understood the concept quickly, my father had a difficult time. He kept typing in "dim sum", instead of an address, which made me realize that maybe some things are not as intuitive as I thought and that I should consider changing my placeholder text or an option to view the app in Chinese (even though my dad is a native English speaker, he might react differently when things are in Chinese).
