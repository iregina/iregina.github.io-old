Be a Host on Airbnb

Welcome to the Airbnb Community

Have extra space in your home? Wonderful! There's a world of opportunity awaiting you.

By becoming a host on Airbnb, you can share your home--anything from a spare bedroom to that igloo or treehouse you've been building in your backard--with travelers eager to experience the place you call home.

Airbnb is a community of passionate entrepreneurs and adventurous travelers looking to build a better world--together. We're proud that hundreds of thousands of hosts around the world share their space and make extra income by doing so.
