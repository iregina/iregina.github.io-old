Blogging

I like the idea of writing and blogging. It's important to write about things to solidify concepts and express yourself. However, at the same time, blogging is a public project. It's meant to be read by others and not meant to be private, which induces a lot of pressure on me. I care a lot about how my project looks and whether it's something meaningful to myself as well as to other -- which puts in a bit of a paralysis. I would like to make the blog easier to use and get to a form in which I am proud of -- I want it to be more like wordpress and less like seperate html projects.
